Delicious Adobo Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Watch How to Make

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, peppercorns, and bay leaves.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the marinade and let it marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in the remaining marinade and water. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  7. Remove the lid and continue cooking until the sauce thickens.
  8. Remove bay leaves before serving.
